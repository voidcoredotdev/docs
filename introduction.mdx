## Getting Started

Follow these steps to make the most of Voidcore Wiki right away.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a topic">
    Use the sidebar or the cards above to navigate to the section that matches what you are working on — Linux, Ubuntu, Networking, or Sysadmin.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for a command or concept">
    Press **Ctrl + K** (or **⌘ K** on macOS) to open the search bar and type any command, keyword, or topic. Results appear instantly across all articles.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow the guide">
    Each article is structured with prerequisites at the top, sequential steps in the middle, and troubleshooting notes at the bottom. Read the prerequisites before running any commands.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bookmark and return">
    Save articles you use frequently. Every page URL is stable and permanent — safe to bookmark or share with your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Voidcore Wiki is maintained by volunteers. If you spot an error, outdated command, or a gap in coverage, consider [contributing](/contributing) — even a small correction helps the whole community.
</Note>
